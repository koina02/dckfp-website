About DCKFP

Shaping Kenya's global diplomatic strategy through research, training, and policy engagement

Our Institution

The Diplomatic College of Kenyan Foreign Policy (DCKFP) is Nairobi's premier institution dedicated to advancing Kenya's global diplomatic strategy. Established in 2025, we serve as a bridge between academic research and practical foreign policy implementation.

Our work focuses on strengthening Kenya's position in regional and international affairs while developing the next generation of diplomatic leaders through innovative training programs and strategic policy engagement.

Our History

Founded through a partnership between the Kenyan Ministry of Foreign Affairs and leading academic institutions, DCKFP builds on Kenya's rich diplomatic tradition dating back to the early years of independence.

Over the years, we have grown to become a recognized center of excellence in African diplomacy and foreign policy analysis, contributing to Kenya's growing influence on the continental and global stage.

Our Mission

To train Kenya's future diplomats and shape evidence-based foreign policy for Africa's geopolitical landscape through rigorous research, innovative training programs, and strategic policy engagement with government and international partners.

Our Vision

To be Africa's leading institution for diplomatic excellence, recognized for developing transformative foreign policy solutions and producing world-class diplomatic professionals who advance Kenya's interests and contribute to global peace and development.
